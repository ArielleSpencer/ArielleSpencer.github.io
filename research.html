ACCESSIBLE THEMED ENVIRONMENTS THROUGH DESIGN

My name is Arielle Spencer and I’m a PhD student in Planning, Design, and the Built Environment at Clemson University studying accessibility in themed entertainment. 

I am currently working on a research project exploring the current accommodation options for guests with disabilities at theme parks. The project will contribute to understanding the current accommodations and will hopefully help shape conversations around future accommodations and designs. For more information about the study, see here, and if you are interested in participating in the study please contact me at ariell4@g.clemson.edu.
